::: content-block__right
### Acumin Pro

This typeface provides accessible styles for use in sidebar text,
captions, and callouts. It is a versatile, modern, sans serif typeface
family designed for maximum versatility, featuring a neutral aesthetic
suitable for both text and display, making it ideal for information
design, branding, and editorial use.
:::
:::
:::
:::

::: content-block__right
### Acumin Pro Condensed

With its stylized weights for special instances, this is a highly
versatile, heavyweight sans serif typeface that features a narrow,
space-efficient width and a strong presence, making it ideal for
high-impact headlines and information design where a modern aesthetic is
required.
:::
:::
:::
:::

::: content-block__right
### Acumin Pro Extra Condensed Black

Primarily used for headlines, this is a highly bold, narrow sans serif
typeface designed for maximum impact in headlines, using an extremely
narrow width and high-density weight to maximize screen or page usage,
while maintaining a modern, clean aesthetic.
:::
:::
:::

::: content-block__right
### Warnock Pro

Warnock Pro is a classic yet contemporary type family that performs a
variety of typographic tasks with elegance. The wide range of weights
available in the Warnock Pro family lends versatility and adaptability
to messaging needs. Warnock Pro Light is perfect for body copy as well
as headlines.

### Oswald Bold

A sans serif font mainly for headlines, it features a condensed
structure with strong, narrow characters, making it ideal for impactful
messages, web design, and high-legibility, space-efficient, and
authoritative branding.

### Roboto

This is a sans serif font family alternative. Known for its modern,
highly legible design, it combines geometric elements with open,
friendly curves, making it ideal for mobile interfaces, web, and digital
branding.

### Crimson Text

This is a serif font family alternative. It is characterized by its
traditional, elegant, and highly legible design, making it ideal for
body text in print, long-form reading, and academic publishing.
